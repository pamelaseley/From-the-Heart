From the Heart

I don't have many words,
But I think from the heart;
For this feeling I'm feeling,
I can never depart.

I don't have much to offer,
But I'm giving it all;
It's the only way I know,
To make sure you stand tall.

It's not about the money,
Or the things you may own;
It's about how you feel inside,
And the things that are shown.

It's about the moments,
That you treasure each day;
When the feeling of love inside you,
Refuses to go away.

It's about the emotions,
That words can't explain;
The feeling in your heart,
When you experience something so plain.

It's about the joy,
That comes from the love within;
It's about the way you feel,
When you learn to truly forgive.

It's about the times,
When you're feeling down;
It's about the courage to rise,
And turn that frown around.

It's about the times,
When you feel all alone;
It's about the strength to carry on,
To make it on your own.

It's about the people,
That help you along the way;
The ones who stay by your side,
Even when you feel on display.

It's about the laughter,
That comes from deep inside;
It's about the way you feel,
When you can't help but smile.

It's about the memories,
That never go away;
It's about the times,
That you just want to stay.

And it's about the love,
That comes from the heart;
It's the only thing that matters,
When it comes down to the start.
